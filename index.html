Happy B'day

Baishnabi

Of all the Three Hundered and Sixty Five odd days in an year,
There is one that reminds,life is not just a rigramole;
The one that reminds us to hope for a brave new year,
Filled with fun and surrounded by people so dear.
The one that reminds us to look up and thank for where we're,
The one that reminds us to look back and smile at where we're,
The one that reminds us to look ahead and prepare,
The one that reminds us to leave behind the dull sadness and aspire.
Without much further ado,Happy Birthday my friend so dear.
